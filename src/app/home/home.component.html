Explore Ireland Web Map Application.
Discover the undiscovered...

Explore Ireland is an interactive web map application that allows users to discover new attractions, activites and locations across the island of Ireland. There is something for everyone to discover, from Hiking to Climbing or Swimming to Fishing, whether you prefer quiet trails or fun group activites, you'll discover something exciting at Explore Ireland.

Activites & Attractions.
Something for everybody..

Explore Ireland is powered by a database of over 5,000 locations and attractions coming from a growing data set from Failte Ireland. This allows us to continually update with new ocations for you to discover. The search function allows you to search by activity or by title to find your next adventure.
Once you save locations you'll be able to view them on an interactive map. The application also gives you live weather reports including weather warnings that are currently in place. This allows you to plan your activity safely, days in advance.

Additonal Information

All of our location and attractions details come from a Failte Ireland database. If you wish learn more about Failte Ireland please follow the link on the right.
Our live weather data comes from OpenWeatherMap, more information for please go to https://openweathermap.org/.
